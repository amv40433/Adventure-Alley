Welcome to Adventure Alley!

Have you ever been reading a story and wished you could help the main character make their decisions? Here at Adventure Alley, you can do exactly that! This is a choose-your-own-adventure website where your choices guide the story. Start your journey by picking an adventure from one of our four categories. As you read, you’ll make decisions that shape the characters’ paths and determine their endings.

Humor

The Christmas Crash Out

The holidays are supposed to be cheerful… but in your family, they’re a battlefield. As the youngest, you’re the prime target for prying questions, passive-aggressive comments, and unsolicited life advice from every direction. This year, you’ve decided you’re done being the quiet one. Armed with snark, strategy, and a mission to become the ultimate comeback king or queen, you’ll navigate dinner disasters, nosy relatives, and awkward gift exchanges. Every choice you make will shape whether you leave the celebration victorious—or hide in the bathroom until dessert.

Romantic Tragedy

Will I Ever Get to Say Goodbye

When Adelyn moves into her new apartment, she’s expecting a fresh start not an unexpected connection. Then she meets him: her mysterious, kind, impossibly charming neighbor who seems to carry a heavy sadness behind his smile. What she doesn’t know is that his time is running out, and everything between them is unfolding against the ticking clock of fate. As Adelyn grows closer to him, you’ll guide her choices whether she pushes forward, pulls away, or uncovers truths she never saw coming. Your decisions may give her the chance to say goodbye… or leave her with a heartbreak she never prepared for.

Mystery

Gone With the Crust

The state fair is buzzing with excitement, and Valerie’s legendary blueberry pies are set to be the star of the baking competition until disaster strikes. Every last pie has vanished without a trace. Enter Detective Charlie: sharp-eyed, sharp-witted, and determined to crack the case before the judging begins. With a growing list of suspects, from jealous competitors to mischievous fair performers, you’ll help Charlie investigate clues, interrogate witnesses, and uncover hidden motives. Can you piece together the truth before the culprit slips away… or worse, wins first place with stolen pastries?

LGBTQ Romance

A Night to Remember

For the first time in your life, you’ve fallen for a woman and tonight is the night you finally get to take her out. Butterflies? Absolutely. A million worries about messing it up? Definitely. As Emily, you’ll step into an evening filled with possibilities: sparks of chemistry, unexpected twists, heartfelt conversations, and choices that could turn a simple date into a life-changing moment. Will nerves get the best of you, or will this night become the beginning of something extraordinary? Your decisions shape every moment and every heartbeat.
