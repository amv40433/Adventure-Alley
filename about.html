About The Book Shelf

Welcome to my bookshelf! I've picked out a few of my favorite books for you to take a look at. These books aren't like other books you've read. Sometimes, they need a little help figuring out what happens next. Select your choice at the bottom of each page to direct the story.
Listed below are a few short descriptions of each book in case you have trouble deciding which one to read first.

Will I ever Get To Say Goodbye?

Genre: Romantic Tragedy

When Adelyne moves into her new apartment, she’s expecting a fresh start, not an unexpected connection. Then she sees him: A tall, broad-shouldered man with short curls the color of coal. From that moment on, she can’t stop wondering who he is. As stolen glances turn into quiet hope, Adelyne must decide whether to take a chance on the connection pulling her closer or risk letting him become just another beautiful stranger who fades away before she ever learns his name.

A Night To Remember

Genre: Romance

Tonight is the night! Your first date with a beautiful woman, and everything feels like it’s riding on these few hours. But first dates are fragile things. Will the spark be strong enough to earn a second date, or will tonight mark the quiet end of a short-lived romance?

Gone with the Crust

Genre: Mystery

The state fair is buzzing with excitement, and Valerie’s legendary shepherd's pies are set to be the star of the baking competition until disaster strikes. Every last pie has vanished without a trace. Enter Detective Charlie: sharp-eyed, sharp-witted, and determined to crack the case before the judging begins. Help Charlie investigate clues, interrogate witnesses, and uncover hidden motives.

The Christmas Crash Out

Genre: Humor

The holidays are supposed to be cheerful… but in your family, they’re a battlefield. As the youngest, you’re the prime target for prying questions, passive-aggressive comments, and unsolicited life advice from every direction. This year, you’ve decided you’re done being the quiet one and armed with snark, strategy, and a mission to become the ultimate comeback king
