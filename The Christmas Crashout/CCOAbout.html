The Christmas Crashout

CHARACTERS

CAMREN:
Early 20s, college student, sarcastic and witty but tired of being the family punching bag.

MOTHER:
Late 40s, CAMREN's overly affectionate mother and a bit oblivious to how smothering she can be.

AUNT BRENDA:
Early 50s, nosy and judgmental, loves to interrogate Camren about his life choices.

GRANDMA:
Mid 70s, traditional and sharp-tongued, often makes cutting remarks under the guise of concern.

COUSIN TINA:
30s, mischievous and enjoys teasing Camren, especially about his college life.

COUSIN DEREK:
Late 20s, playful and supportive of Tina's teasing, but has a good heart.

UNCLE:
Early 60s, quiet and observant, rarely speaks but his presence is felt.
