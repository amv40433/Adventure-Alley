Scene Four

CAMREN:
Isn’t that your third divorce?

(GRANDMA practically leaps out of her chair, waving her hands frantically.)

GRANDMA (screaming):
How dare you say that at my table?! That is the rudest thing I’ve ever heard in my entire life! I cannot believe you!

(The room erupts in chaos, and everyone begins talking over one another)

COUSIN TINA (snapping, pointing at Camren):
See! This is exactly what I’m talking about!

COUSIN DEREK (jumping up, furious):
Yeah! How can you just sit there and—

AUNT BRENDA (standing, voice high-pitched, slamming her hands):
Camren! That is entirely unacceptable! What has gotten into you!

MOTHER (gritting teeth, flailing hands, and getting everyonones attention):
Everyone! Everyone! Sit down! This is a dinner table, not a battlefield!

UNCLE (calm, slow, eyes on CAMREN):
He’s not wrong.

(The room freezes for a beat. Then chaos erupts louder than before. Shouts, gesturing, and finger-pointing fill the air.)

COUSIN TINA (yelling at UNCLE):
Not wrong?! How can you say that?

COUSIN DEREK (to Camren, frantic):
You can’t just say stuff like that!

AUNT BRENDA (glaring at UNCLE):
Exactly! What is wrong with you?!

GRANDMA (gasping, clutching pearls again, frantic):
I can’t believe this! This is disgraceful!

MOTHER (desperate, voice breaking):
Camren… please… I think It's time for you to leave.

    CAMREN:


  • I think it's time Santa brought y'all some manners.
